T family (Burstable – very popular)
==========================================
Used for low to medium workloads:

| Instance  | vCPU | RAM  | Use case           |
| --------- | ---- | ---- | ------------------ |
| t2.micro  | 1    | 1 GB | Free tier, testing |
| t3.micro  | 2    | 1 GB | Dev, small apps    |
| t3.small  | 2    | 2 GB | APIs, web servers  |
| t3.medium | 2    | 4 GB | Light production   |
| t4g.micro | 2    | 1 GB | ARM-based, cheaper |

Best for:
Dev/Test
Small web apps
Jenkins, Ansible controller
Bastion hosts
